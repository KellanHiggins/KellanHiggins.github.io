What?

An Indie Gaming Company in Vancouver. We specialize in online, realistic simulation games on Windows, Mac OSX and Linux.

We are going to make some awesome games for the the most dedicated gamers! First a zombie game, then probably a realistic space shooter. Finally, a pogo stick simulator. The most realistic yet!

Regardless, we want to make games you love. So check us out in a few months and we'll have a few more updates, maybe even some demo videos (or a playable concept demo).

Have fun!

- 8 Bit Goose


Who?



Kellan is the founder of 8Bit Goose Games and before 8Bit Goose, Kellan worked at a SilkStart, a SaaS membership management platform. He's been involved in the Startup Scene in Vancouver since 2011, managing client relations, planning projects, performing user feedback studies and programming in C#.

Kellan has diverse experience, serving on both the development and business side of startups. No longer excited about the B2B space, Kellan taught himself to program, left SilkStart in early 2014 and founded 8Bit Goose.

Before entering the Vancouver Startup scene, Kellan served as Coordinating Editor for the Ubyssey Student Newspaper, improving the production of the 90 year old paper and ushering the paper into a new golden age of digital production

In his off time, Kellan plays Ultimate and is currently addicted to Kerbal Space Program. He also is really good at Vancouver history and loves to take photos.
